FAR-WESTERN UNIVERSITY

End-Term Examination-2076

Database Management System(CSIT.223)

Faculty: Science and Technology (CSIT)

Level: Undergraduate

Semester: Fourth

Full Marks: 80

Time: 2hrs. 40 minutes

Candidates are required to give their answers in their own words as far as practicable. The figures in the margin indicate full marks.

Group-A

Attempt any six questions.
6 x 8 = 48
  1. What is join in DBMS? Explain outer join with example.
  2. Discuss the desirable properties of transaction. Explain state diagram of a transaction with example.
  3. Explain three level architecture of DBMS with example.
  4. What is concurrency control? Describe Timestamp ordering protocol in brief.
  5. What is database recovery? What happens at checkpoint? How is a database recovered using checkpoints?
  6. Illustrate the functional dependency with example.
  7. How is deadlock detected? Discuss deadlock avoidance scheme.
  8. Write short note (any two)
    1. ER diagram
    2. Data Independence
    3. Triggers

    9. Group-B

    Attempt any two questions.
    2 x 16 = 32
  9. What is normalization? Why is it needed? How do you convert 2NF into 3NF and describe with example?
  10. What do you mean by integrity constraints? Mention its importance in DBMS and explain various types of integrity constraints with practical example in MySQL.
  11. Describe the following terms relating to DBMS.
    1. Generalization
    2. DBA
    3. Weak entity sets
    4. Projection operation
