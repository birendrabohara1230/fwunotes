End-Term Examination-2079

System Analysis and Design (CSIT.225)

Faculty: Science and Technology (CSIT)

Level: Undergraduate

Semester: Fourth

Full Marks: 100

Time: 3hrs

Candidates are required to give their answers in their own words as far as practicable. The figures in the margin indicate full marks.

Group-A

Attempt all questions. (Very Short Questions)
8 x 3 = 24

  2. Define the term life cycle and methodology.
  3. Define feasibility. Explain operational feasibility.
  4. What is functional and non-functional requirement?
  5. What is relationship? Mention types of relationship in ER diagram.
  6. Why DFD is necessary in process modeling?
  7. Describe system implementation.
  8. Define unit testing and system testing.
