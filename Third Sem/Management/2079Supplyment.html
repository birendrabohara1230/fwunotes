End-Term Examination-2079

Introduction to Management (Supplementary) (CSIT.213)

Faculty: Science and Technology (CSIT)

Level: Undergraduate

Semester: Third

Full Marks: 100

Time: 3hrs

Candidates are required to give their answers in their own words as far as practicable. The figures in the margin indicate full marks.

Group-A

Attempt all questions. (Very short questions)
8 x 3 = 24
  1. Why organization are established? Justify your answer.
  2. State features of management.
  3. Why planning is known as primary function of management?
  4. Define human resource management.
  5. State the functions of top-level management.
  6. Define social responsibility.
  7. What is SWOT analysis?
  8. Define the term 'Unity of Command'.

    5 x 8 = 40
